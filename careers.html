Culture
At CIMERWA, culture isn’t just something – it’s everything. We are a private company which started as a State owned with a rich heritage of making both the highest quality cement and the finest talent in the manufacturing industry. Ours is a culture deeply rooted in decades of passionate and unwavering commitment to excellence.
At CIMERWA, we’re proud of our heritage. But we keep our eyes focused ahead. We know that our future success lies not in where we’ve been, but in where we’re going.

“We really want every employee to think of his or her position in the company as more than a job — as a gratifying enjoyable part of his or her life.”
We value the following qualities in CIMERWA people:

  • Loyalty
  • Hard working
  • Tenacity
  • Passion for cement and cement products
  • Motivation
Vacancies
Our team prizes the right people to achieve the right results. If you would like to explore current opportunities with our company, please view our current vacancies here. As an Affirmative Action and Equal Opportunity Employer, we apply a uniform online applicant tracking system (hiring platform) process to insure candidates are properly and transparently considered.  Candidates should apply for each individual position they desire to be considered using the method stated in each posting.  Please note that we only accept applications for currently open positions; apply each application only to that specified position.
Talent diversity and inclusion
We become greater by sharing in and embracing the richness of our differences. Each of us brings new perspectives, experiences, skills, abilities and knowledge to our everyday tasks. Although we may be different in race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, or any other status, we believe the presence of these differences to be representative of a strong and positive team.
At no time do we consider these differences in any employment opportunity or condition, and all candidates that are qualified for any opportunity which we have currently available are encouraged and welcomed to apply through our established application processes without concern regarding their differences. CIMERWA embraces and welcomes diversity, is a drug-free environment and actively promotes social cohesion.
Internships
CIMERWA offers non-paid internship opportunities comprised of meaningful and relevant real-world challenges to engage and develop. As internship opportunities become available, candidates can explore their availability through either postings as noted on our Careers page or through our partner training institutions listed below. Likewise, new university graduates are encouraged to explore and visit. We sign Memorandum of Understanding (MoUs) with training institutions whose students are considered for internship opportunities. Note that no individual application from students is allowed, partners training institutions are the only ones allowed to submit the applications for their students.
