Buy, Build and Win CIMERWA Promotion
  
TERMS AND CONDITIONS
  
Please find below our terms and conditions:
The following terms and conditions apply to the Buy Build and Win Promotion (“Promotion’’) and all participants in the promotion are deemed to have read, understood and accepted the same:
1. Promotion Period
The promotion is valid from Tuesday, 12th September 2016 to Friday 4th December, 2016 both days inclusive (‘’the Promotion Period’’)
2. Eligibility
a. The promotion is open to all retailers that sell, and consumers that buy CIMERWA cement except employees, both permanent and casual, of CIMERWA LTD and their immediate families, CIMERWA distributors and their employees, advertising agencies, promotional agencies, assurance firm(s) and anyone professionally connected with the promotion.
b. The competition is open to all Rwandan residents (except those indicated in clause 2) who are 18 years or older and have valid Identity Documents.
3. Definitions
a. Retailer – a registered trading entity within Rwanda that is licensed to sell
b. Consumer – a person purchasing cement to go and use as an end user
c. CIMERWA distributor – an entity appointed by CIMERWA to distribute CIMERWA cement
d. Agency – Cube Communications
4. Entry into the promotion
a. How a consumer can enter:
i. You will qualify for entry into the weekly draw for a minimum of five (5) CIMERWA cement bags purchased per sale transaction and evidenced by a valid EBM receipt or CIMERWA Coupon number.
ii. To submit your entry, dial *750# and follow instructions.
b. How a retailer can enter:
i. Register your entity by dialing *750# from your phone and follow instructions.
ii. Display your entry number in the shop for consumers to include your store as they register their entries when they have purchased CIMERWA cement from your store.
iii. You will qualify for one entry into the weekly draw for every five (5) CIMERWA cement bags purchased from your shop per sale transaction evidenced by a valid EBM receipt or CIMERWA Coupon number.
iv. You can also facilitate entries, through your registered telephone numbers on the mobile platform, for qualifying consumers if they are not able to do so themselves.
Please note entries to the promotion can only be sent through the local network providers MTN; TIGO and Airtel.
5. Prizes
By entering the promotion, you stand to win the prizes as follows:
a. Weekly prizes for the first 11 draws will be as follows
i. Two retailers will receive a cash prize of 250,000 Rwf each upon verification of their entry
ii. Two retailers will receive a construction goods voucher worth 250,000 Rwf each upon verification of their entry to give to CIMERWA consumers. These goods may vary week to week.
iii. Six retailers will receive airtime worth 10,000 Rwf each upon verification of their entry
iv. Two consumers will receive a cash prize of 250,000 Rwf each upon verification of their entry
v. Two consumers will receive a goods voucher worth 250,000 Rwf each upon verification of their entry to give to CIMERWA consumers. These goods may vary week to week.
vi. Six consumers will receive airtime worth 10,000 Rwf each upon verification of their
b. The final 12th draw will be as follows:
i. One retailer will receive a Toyota Hilux Single Cabin pick-up
ii. One consumer will receive a Toyota Hilux Single Cabin pick-up

a. Draws will be done as follows:

b. All draws will be held at CIMERWA office in Kimihurura, Kigali, Rwanda
c. CIMERWA reserves the right to conduct draws on any other day within the promotion period for any prizes not validly won or that are unclaimed.
d. A Deloitte Rwanda Limited representative, Call Rwanda representative and CIMERWA marketing team representative MUST be present at each draw witnessed by the Agency representative.
7. Draw Mechanics
a. The winner(s) will be randomly selected electronically during the draws. Any decisions made on the winner by CIMERWA shall be final and binding.
b. A selected entry shall be declared to have won upon verification of a valid purchase and an original valid EBM receipt or CIMERWA Coupon shall be required as proof of purchase.
8. Notification of Winners
a. Winners will be contacted only through the following number: 0733110110
b. For the avoidance of doubt, missed calls and calls diverted to voice mail will not be deemed to have been answered for purposes of the promotion
9. Prize presentation
a. Airtime prizes will be sent to the winners phone directly from an electronic platform
b. Weekly cash & goods prizes will be presented to winners in person by a team comprised of an Agency representative and CIMERWA marketing team representative.
10. Proof of identification (National Identification Card / Passport) will be required when claiming the prizes. Proof of purchase may also be required
11. Winners will be announced on television and radio.
12. The draw selection results are final; no correspondences whatsoever will be entered into.
13. You will be automatically disqualified should you not have proof of purchase i.e. EBM receipts or CIMERWA Coupon as proof that CIMERWA cement was purchased or sold when requested.
14. CIMERWA reserves the right to disqualify or discontinue any consumer or retailer found violated the terms and conditions of this promotion. Fraud charges may also be brought against him/her.
15. Entry into the promotion will start every week after the weekly draw until the 12th draw at which point the promotion will end.
16. The visuals of prizes on point of sale & marketing material are for representation purposes only. CIMERWA reserves the right to supply the same or similar prize if the exact one is not available from respective suppliers at time of winning.
17. If upon notification the selected winner of any prize does not claim the prize thirty (30) days after the end of the promotion he/she will be deemed to have forfeited the same and the prize will be surrendered to CIMERWA.
18. Prizes are not transferable or negotiable.
19. CIMERWA reserves the right to withdraw any prizes to be awarded to any person suspected or implicated of fraudulent activities or attempts thereof related to the promotion. Fraud charges may also be brought to him/her.
20. Any personal information collected by CIMERWA during the competition will not be shared with any third party without the participants’ permission.
21. By agreeing to participate in the promotion, all winners of the various prizes agree that their name, and picture can be made public through any media as CIMERWA may choose (including but not limited to internet) and that they will further agree to participate in any public relations and marketing activities relating to the promotion without any monetary consideration being required.
22. CIMERWA reserves the right to suspend or terminate the Promotion or amend these terms and conditions at any time for any valid technical or commercial reason. Any amendment to these terms and conditions will be published in a daily newspaper.
23. Call our toll free number on 3222 for any queries.
